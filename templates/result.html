What These Numbers Mean

📏 BMI (Body Mass Index): A measure of body fat based on height & weight. A healthy range is 18.5 - 24.9.

🔥 BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate): Calories needed for basic body functions at rest.

⚡ TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure): The total calories burned daily, including activity.

⚖️ IBW (Ideal Body Weight): The healthy weight range for your height.

❤️ MHR (Maximum Heart Rate): The max safe heart rate during exercise, based on age.
